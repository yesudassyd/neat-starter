Dr. Naveed Nazir Shah is a professor of pulmonology and an interventional pulmonologist having an experience of over 20 years in treatment of chest diseases. After completing his post graduation in pulmonology he has done European Respiratory Fellowship in Interventional Pulmonology front University of Ancona, Italy and also a Fellowship in Pulmonary and Critical Care Medicine, Rosewell Park Cancer Institute Buffalo, New York, USA. He consults patients at BreathEasy at SanatNagar and ChestCare at Zukura.
